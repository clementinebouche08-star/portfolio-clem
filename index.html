TIPE: Evaporation dynamics of microdroplets on a solid substrate

During the final preparatory course examinations, we must present personal initiative projects under supervision (TIPE). I did mine of the evaporation of droplets: During the whole last year of CPGE, I studied the evaporation through a microscope with this question: What are the mechanisms of evaporation in a droplet, and what conditions can cause evaporation to vary? In order to respond to this question, I wanted to: Conduct a statistical study of the evaporation time of a drop, Compare the curves showing the evolution of the radius of a drop for different drop sizes and identify the different evaporation models, Determine where the coffee stain forms. At the end of the year we needed to do an oral presentation, in addition to teaching me how to manage my work by following objectives, it taught me how to summarise my results in order to present them orally.
